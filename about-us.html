About Anupam Mondal

Renowned freelancer based in Kolkata, West Bengal, serving clients worldwide since 2016

Hailing from Kolkata, West Bengal, I embarked on my professional journey as a computer teacher/trainer in 2005. Over the years, I've evolved into a dedicated professional well-versed in various technologies.

I now provide valuable consultations to businesses and organizations, aiding them in their website growth on digital platforms as a full-stack developer. My prowess in the field is evident through the glowing reviews from happy clients worldwide.

Before transitioning to freelancing in 2016, I honed my skills in the IT and education sectors as a Faculty/Trainer. Additionally, armed with both basic and advanced knowledge of Finance, Accounting, and Taxation, I bring a holistic approach to my projects.
